        <h3> What is an iced Americano really? </h3></font>
<pre>
The Americano originated in Italy in the 1950s.

American soldiers who were stationed there after World War II, found the flavor of espresso to be too intense.
So they’d often order espresso diluted with hot water, which resulted in a drink that resembled
the brewed coffee they were accustomed to at home.
</pre>
<font color="Brown" >
 <caption><b>The Ingredients :</b></caption>
</font>

         <h3><b>Procedure : </b></h3>
         </font>
         <ul>
          <li> Pour cold water into a cup. </li>
          <li> Add espresso. </li>
          <li>  Top drink with ice.  </li>
        </ul>
